Customer Birthday
This template will automatically trigger a birthday sequence for a customer on their birthday. Use it to make each of your customers feel special on their special day
Win-Back Sequence
This template automatically enrolls a customer who has stopped visiting your classes or appointments into a sequence. Use it to make your customers feel like they are missed by you and your staff, or offer discounts on memberships to win them back
Abandoned Checkout Lead Conversion
This template is designed to convert potential customers who were about to purchase something but then didn't. Any non-customer who abandons one of your checkout pages will be added and sent a series of emails and SMS messages inviting them to reconsider their purchase and visit your business.
Lead Conversion
This template features a series of emails, text messages and phone call follow-ups that motivate potential customers to buy your intro offer. The sequence is structured to ensure you're maximizing your conversion rate potential from leads that are generated via paid social ads, your website and QR codes.
Convert Referrals to Members
This template includes a high-converting set of sequences that (a) ensure customer referrals make their first booking, (b) follow up until they return for a 2nd visit and/or purchase of your intro offer (c) turn loyal customers into influencers for your studio by encouraging them to share their referral link after hitting class visit milestones
New Customer
This template will enter a new customer who hasn't purchased an intro offer into a new customer nurture sequence, intended to make each new customer feel welcomed.
Intro offer funnel
This template will create a high-converting set of sequences for your intro offers, with the goal of getting each customer that purchases an intro offer to feel welcomed and to ultimately end up purchasing another membership after their intro offer expires
Customer Milestones
This template will automatically enter customers into celebratory sequences when they hit visit milestones. Use it to celebrate your customers' loyalty to your business, and reward them for continuing to come back!
