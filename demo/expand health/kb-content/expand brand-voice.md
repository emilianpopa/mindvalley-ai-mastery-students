# Expand Health – Brand Voice (Cape Town)



Expand Health – Brand Voice (Cape Town, South Africa)



Who We Are

Expand Health is a longevity and health optimisation hub in Cape Town.

We combine diagnostics, evidence-based medical care, and advanced wellness therapies to help people feel better now and protect their future health.



Core Principles



Preventive and proactive: We focus on early detection, prevention, and optimisation – not just treating disease.



Personalised: Every client gets a tailored plan based on their goals, data, and lifestyle.



Science-led: We use clinically grounded diagnostics, therapies, and protocols.



Human and empathetic: We speak like real people, listen deeply, and meet clients where they are.



Empowering: We give people tools, education, and support so they can own their health journey.



Tone of Voice



Clear and to the point – no fluff, no jargon if we can avoid it.



Warm, encouraging, and respectful.



Confident but not arrogant.



Curious and optimistic about what is possible for healthspan and longevity.



Calm and reassuring when people feel overwhelmed or unwell.



How We Speak



Use simple, precise language: “high blood sugar” instead of “hyperglycaemia” unless needed.



Explain the “why” behind every recommendation (tests, protocols, therapies).



Avoid fear-based messaging. We focus on opportunity, not panic.



Avoid hard-selling; we recommend, explain benefits, and let the client decide.



Use examples and real-life scenarios to make concepts concrete.



Words And Phrases We Like



Longevity



Health optimisation



Healthspan



Personalized protocol



Preventive and proactive care



Root-cause approach



Data-driven



Nervous system regulation



Cellular repair and regeneration



Sustainable lifestyle changes



Words And Phrases We Avoid



Miracle cure, quick fix, detox gimmicks



Overpromising (“reverse ageing in 10 days”)



Aggressive sales language (“must buy now”, “only today”)



Generic wellness clichés that mean nothing
