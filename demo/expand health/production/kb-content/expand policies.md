# Expand Health Cape Town – Policies



Expand Health Cape Town – Key Policies



General

1.1 Expand Health Cape Town is a longevity and health optimisation clinic offering both medical and wellness services.

1.2 We operate as a cash-based practice. We can provide invoices for reimbursement, but we do not guarantee medical aid coverage.

1.3 Our protocols are designed to complement, not replace, the advice of your primary care physician or specialist.



Bookings And Appointments

2.1 Appointments can be made via our online booking system, WhatsApp, phone, or email.

2.2 First-time clients are encouraged to book a Health Insights assessment or medical consultation before starting complex therapies.

2.3 Please arrive 10–15 minutes before your first session to complete intake forms and orientation.



Cancellation And No-Show Policy

3.1 We require at least 24 hours’ notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment.

3.2 Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the session may be charged a late cancellation fee or the full session amount.

3.3 No-shows (not arriving and not notifying us) may be charged the full session fee and may need to prepay future bookings.



Payment Terms

4.1 Payment is due at the time of service, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4.2 Accepted payment methods include card payments and bank transfers; cash acceptance depends on clinic policy.

4.3 Packages and memberships must be paid upfront or via an agreed payment schedule.

4.4 Package sessions are non-transferable unless authorised by management.



Expiry Of Packages And Memberships

5.1 Packages and session bundles have an expiry date, typically 3–6 months from purchase depending on the package.

5.2 Memberships renew on a monthly basis unless cancelled in advance according to the membership agreement.

5.3 Unused sessions may expire at the end of the validity period and are not automatically carried forward.



Medical Safety And Contraindications

6.1 Certain therapies (for example IVs and hyperbaric oxygen) require medical screening and, where appropriate, a doctor’s consultation.

6.2 You must inform us of all medications, supplements, allergies, and medical conditions before starting any protocol.

6.3 We may decline or postpone treatment if we believe it is unsafe or not in your best interest.

6.4 Pregnant or breastfeeding clients, or those with uncontrolled medical conditions, must discuss suitability with our team before booking.



Informed Consent

7.1 Before receiving therapies, you will be asked to read and sign an informed consent form.

7.2 The consent form explains benefits, risks, and alternatives.

7.3 You may withdraw consent at any time, but sessions already completed or late-cancelled remain chargeable.



Confidentiality And Data

8.1 All client information, health data, and records are kept confidential and handled according to applicable privacy laws.

8.2 We may use de-identified data to improve protocols and outcomes.

8.3 We will not share your identifiable information with third parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law.



Behaviour And Use Of Space

9.1 We aim to maintain a calm, respectful environment. Aggressive, abusive, or disruptive behaviour will not be tolerated.

9.2 Please keep your phone on silent and respect the privacy of other clients.

9.3 Children are welcome only if they have an appointment or if prior arrangements have been made with the team.



Results And Expectations

10.1 Individual results vary. We cannot guarantee specific outcomes.

10.2 Our role is to provide high-quality diagnostics, therapies, and guidance; your results depend on multiple factors including lifestyle and adherence to the protocol.

10.3 We encourage open communication. If something does not feel right, or if you are not seeing expected progress, please speak to our team so we can adjust your plan.



Updates To Policies

11.1 These policies may be updated over time.

11.2 The latest version will be available from the clinic or on our website.

11.3 By receiving services at Expand Health Cape Town, you agree to the current version of these policies.
