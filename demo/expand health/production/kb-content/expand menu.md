Assessments And Consultations



1.1 Health Insights Assessment



Entry point for most clients.



Includes intake, body composition analysis, discussion of goals, and review of risk factors and lifestyle.



May include mineral and heavy metal screening and other rapid diagnostics depending on package.



Outcome: a personalised health optimisation plan and recommendation for wellness or medical pathway.



1.2 Medical Consultation – Longevity Doctor



In-depth clinical assessment with our integrative / longevity-focused doctors.



Review of current symptoms, history, medications, and previous labs.



Request or interpretation of blood tests and advanced diagnostics.



Design of a medical protocol which may include therapies at Expand Health and external investigations.



1.3 Nutrition Consultation



One-on-one session with our dietitian or nutrition specialist.



Focus on metabolic health, gut health, inflammation, and practical meal strategies.



Personalised nutrition plan and supplement guidance.

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT)



Sessions in a pressurised chamber with increased oxygen.



Used for recovery, cognitive support, inflammation reduction, and tissue healing.



Duration usually 60–90 minutes per session.



Often prescribed as a series (e.g. 10–20 sessions) within a broader protocol.



Red Light Therapy



Full-body or targeted red and near-infrared light exposure.



Supports mitochondrial energy production, skin health, muscle recovery, and mood.



Sessions typically 10–20 minutes.



Often combined with other therapies (e.g. before or after training, IVs, or sauna).



Sauna And Cold Exposure



Full-spectrum infrared or traditional sauna sessions.



Cold plunge or cold shower protocols where available.



Used for detox support, cardiovascular conditioning, nervous system resilience, and recovery.



May be offered as “sauna and cold circuit” packages.



PEMF And Nervous System Technologies



Pulsed electromagnetic field (PEMF) sessions for circulation, cellular energy, and pain support.



Brain and nervous-system targeted devices for stress, focus, and sleep optimisation.



Often combined into stress recovery or sleep improvement protocols.



Protocols And Packages



We structure most services into personalised protocols rather than one-off sessions.



Examples (names can be adjusted to your internal Restore / Revitalize / Regenerate tiers):



7.1 Restore – Foundation



Focus: Rebalance, reduce stress, support sleep and energy.



Typical duration: 4–6 weeks.



May include: Health Insights, a basic IV series, red light, sauna and cold sessions, and nervous system support.



7.2 Revitalize – Performance And Metabolic Health



Focus: Metabolic reset, performance, resilience.



Typical duration: 8–12 weeks.



May include: Health Insights, doctor consult, targeted labs, personalised IVs, HBOT, red light, PEMF, nutrition coaching.



7.3 Regenerate – Deep Longevity And Repair



Focus: Cellular repair, brain and cardiovascular health, complex or chronic cases.



Typical duration: 12+ weeks.



May include: extended HBOT blocks, advanced IV protocols, multi-system diagnostics, and close medical follow-up.



Memberships



Monthly membership options providing a set number of sessions per modality and member-only pricing.



Ideal for clients who want ongoing maintenance once their initial protocol is complete.



Examples: “Wellness Membership”, “Performance Membership”, “Longevity Membership”.



Events And Workshops



Small-group sessions on topics such as fasting, sleep optimisation, breathwork, nervous system regulation, and nutrition.



Often include a practical component and access to selected therapies.
