# Treatment Plan

## Top 3 Findings
1. Vitamin D deficiency
2. Low-normal B12 levels
3. Low ferritin (iron) levels

## Top 3 Recommendations
1. Address nutrient deficiencies through targeted supplementation and dietary changes
2. Implement lifestyle modifications to improve sleep, reduce stress, and increase physical activity
3. Utilize ExpandHealth modalities to support energy production and overall well-being

## Core Protocol Breakdown

### MONTH 1: Weeks 1-4
**Supplements:**
- Vitamin D3: 5,000 IU daily
- Methylcobalamin (B12): 1,000 mcg daily
- Iron (as ferrous bisglycinate): 30 mg daily

**ExpandHealth Modalities:**
- High-Dose Vitamin C IV: 1x per week
- Red Light Therapy: 20 minutes daily

**Lifestyle:**
- Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night
- Engage in 30 minutes of moderate exercise (e.g., walking, yoga) 3 times per week
- Practice stress-reducing techniques (e.g., meditation, deep breathing) for 15 minutes daily

### MONTH 2: Weeks 5-8
**Supplements:**
- Vitamin D3: 5,000 IU daily
- Methylcobalamin (B12): 1,000 mcg daily
- Iron (as ferrous bisglycinate): 30 mg daily

**ExpandHealth Modalities:**
- High-Dose Vitamin C IV: 1x per week
- Red Light Therapy: 20 minutes daily
- Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT): 1 session per week

**Lifestyle:**
- Maintain sleep schedule and exercise routine
- Incorporate 30 minutes of restorative activities (e.g., reading, journaling) daily
- Gradually increase physical activity to 45 minutes, 4 times per week

### MONTH 3: Weeks 9-12
**Supplements:**
- Vitamin D3: 5,000 IU daily
- Methylcobalamin (B12): 1,000 mcg daily
- Iron (as ferrous bisglycinate): 30 mg daily

**ExpandHealth Modalities:**
- High-Dose Vitamin C IV: 1x per month
- Red Light Therapy: 20 minutes daily
- Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT): 1 session per month

**Lifestyle:**
- Maintain sleep schedule and exercise routine
- Incorporate 1 hour of restorative activities daily
- Maintain physical activity at 45-60 minutes, 4-5 times per week

## Weekly Modality Rhythm
- Monday: Red Light Therapy
- Tuesday: HBOT (every other week)
- Wednesday: Red Light Therapy
- Thursday: Red Light Therapy
- Friday: High-Dose Vitamin C IV (Week 1, 5, 9)
- Saturday: Red Light Therapy
- Sunday: Rest

## Expected Outcomes

**Week 4 Targets:**
- Improved energy levels
- Better sleep quality
- Reduced brain fog

**Week 8 Targets:**
- Normalized vitamin D, B12, and ferritin levels
- Increased physical activity
- Improved stress management

**Week 12 Targets:**
- Sustained energy levels and overall well-being
- Ability to maintain healthy lifestyle habits
- Reduced need for ExpandHealth modalities

## Recommended Testing
- Repeat vitamin D, B12, and ferritin levels at 4 and 12 weeks
- Consider thyroid panel and comprehensive metabolic panel at 12 weeks
