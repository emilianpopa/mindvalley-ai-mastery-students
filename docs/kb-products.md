# Expand Health – Products & Services Overview

## 1. Diagnostics

### Health Insights Package
A foundational assessment including:
- Oligoscan (minerals + heavy metals)
- InBody body composition
- BioWell energy assessment
- Blood pressure, HRV, lifestyle intake
- AI-generated report

### Health 360 Package
A comprehensive medical assessment including:
- Advanced blood panels
- DNA / GI testing
- Biological age (GlycanAge or TruAge)
- Functional medicine consultation
- Full AI-driven health plan

## 2. Protocol Tiers (1–3 Months)

### Restore (Low Touch)
1–2 sessions per week
Focus areas: foundational recovery, detoxification, sleep, energy
Modalities: red light, PEMF, sauna, hydration, compression

### Revitalize (Medium Touch)
2–3 sessions per week
Adds: IV therapy, HBOT (soft chamber), health coaching
Focus: metabolic health, inflammation, mitochondrial function

### Regenerate (High Touch)
3–4 sessions per week
Adds: advanced medical therapies, peptides (clinician-guided), neurotherapies
Focus: regeneration, performance, accelerated recovery
