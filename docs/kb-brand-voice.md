# Expand Health – Brand Voice Guidelines

## Brand Personality
Expand Health communicates with clarity, authority, and warmth. The voice combines scientific rigor with a human, optimistic tone—empowering individuals to take control of their health in practical, data-driven ways.

### Core Attributes
- Professional
- Warm and approachable
- Evidence-based
- Visionary, but grounded in real operations
- Empowering and human-centric

---

## Tone Guidelines

### 1. Clear and Confident
Communicate complex concepts in simple, precise language.

### 2. Science-Driven but Approachable
Use evidence, diagnostics, and practitioner insight—but avoid heavy jargon.

### 3. Empathetic and Supportive
Acknowledge client emotions, motivations, and aspirations.

### 4. Action-Oriented
Provide specific steps, guidance, and next actions.

---

## Preferred Vocabulary
Use terms that reflect Expand Health's model and philosophy:
- Personalized health programs
- Preventive care
- Health optimization
- Longevity & vitality
- Data-driven insights
- Protocols & assessments
- Wellness + medical integration
- Foundational systems, metabolic health, nervous system balance

---

## Language to Avoid
- Overpromises or miracle claims
- Generic wellness clichés
- Fear-based messaging
- Vague or hype-driven language

---

## Writing Principles

### 1. Clarity First
Break down complex topics into digestible explanations.

### 2. Authority Through Expertise
Reference diagnostics, medical oversight, and operational experience.

### 3. Human-Centered
Speak directly to client goals—energy, performance, aging well, prevention.

### 4. Consistency Across Channels
Whether writing for:
- Website
- Instagram
- Clinical SOP
- Sales material
- Partner decks

…the tone must stay aligned.

---

## Example Voice (Short)
"Your journey begins with a clear understanding of your biology. Using advanced diagnostics and science-backed protocols, we guide you step-by-step to restore energy, balance your systems, and optimize long-term vitality."
