## Mission
Expand Health empowers individuals to optimize their healthspan through preventative, personalized, and connected longevity programs. We integrate advanced diagnostics, medical expertise, and AI-driven insights to deliver measurable improvements in vitality, performance, and long-term well-being.

## Our Model
Expand Health operates at the intersection of medical care, wellness, and technology. Our ecosystem includes:

### 1. Longevity Clinics
Premium in-person hubs offering:
- Medical consultations
- Diagnostic testing
- Advanced wellness therapies
- Personalized health protocols

Current clinics operate in:
- Cape Town, South Africa
- Bucharest, Romania

Additional territories under development in Asia and the Middle East.

### 2. Expand Labs (AI Platform)
A proprietary software system powering:
- Diagnostic interpretation
- AI-generated protocols
- Patient engagement
- Clinic workflows, bookings, and CRM
- Analytics and reporting

### 3. Longevity Hospitality & Home Wellness
Solutions for:
- Hotels & resorts
- Residences & developments
- At-home biohacking setups

Designed to embed longevity seamlessly into lifestyle environments.
