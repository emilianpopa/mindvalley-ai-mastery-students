## 1. Clinical Safety & Medical Governance
- All medical procedures are supervised by licensed physicians.
- Protocols follow IFM standards and conventional medical safety norms.
- Informed consent is mandatory before any medical intervention.
- Diagnostic interpretation must be conducted only by qualified clinicians.

## 2. Wellness & Device Safety
All device-based treatments require:
- Pre-screening and contraindication checks
- Supervision by trained operators
- Regular equipment calibration and maintenance logs

Devices include: HBOT, cryotherapy, red light, PEMF, sauna, lymphatic compression, neurotherapies.

## 3. Data Protection & Privacy
- Strict compliance with GDPR and equivalent standards.
- Access to client data is permission-based and role-specific.
- No client information is shared without written consent.
- Expand Labs platform encrypts and secures all health data.

## 4. Customer Experience Standards
- Every client begins with a **Health Insights** assessment.
- Personalized protocols must be rooted in diagnostics.
- Follow-ups must occur at pre-defined intervals.
- Membership clients receive priority support and scheduling.

## 5. Ethical Sales & Communication
- No upselling non-essential treatments.
- All recommendations must be justified clinically or appropriately wellness-based.
- Staff must provide transparent pricing and clear expectations.

## 6. Cleanliness & Facility Standards
- Treatment rooms sanitized between each session.
- IV room follows sterile clinical standards.
- All linens, towels, disposables replaced after each use.
- Daily facility checks documented in operational logs.

## 7. Team Conduct & Professionalism
- Staff must maintain professional, kind, and supportive interactions.
- Confidentiality extends beyond clinic premises.
- Team members must be punctual and comply fully with SOPs.
